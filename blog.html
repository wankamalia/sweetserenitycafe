    New Recipes: Italian Tiramisu

    Let's bake!

    To make an Italian Tiramisu, you will need ingredients like ladyfinger biscuits, espresso, mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, and cocoa powder. Here is a special simplified recipe from Sweet Serenity Cafe. Firstly, you have to brew strong the espresso and let it cool. Next, separate the egg yolks from whited and beat the egg yolks with sugar until creamy. Then add mascarpone and mix well. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form and gently fold the egg whites into the mascarpone mixture. Dip the ladyfingers into the cooled espresso and layer them in a dish. Next, spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers and repeat the layers, ending with a layer of mascarpone. Refrigerate for a few hours or overnight then before serving, dust it with cocoa powder. Enjoy your homemade Italian Tiramisu!
